SBO offers several trips on the Salmon River. Based out of Riggins we take do anything from local half-day trips to week long excursions far away from civilization.

This is the quickest trip available and it has the most rapids within 2-4 hours. Half-day trips and full-day trips are both available for this approximately 35 mile stretch.

This 3-4 day stretch of river takes out in Clarkston, Washington with several slot canyons and big rapids in between. The canyons get wicked hot but that makes the rapids even more refreshing.

It's a week long river trip far away from civilization and cell service, but the views are breathtaking. The green present there creates a stark contrast between the Main Salmon and the other two stretches of river.
